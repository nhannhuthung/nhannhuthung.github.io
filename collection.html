Remark

I started my collection with only coins and I continue collecting them until now. However, since there are too many - more than 650 coins, I couldn't put all of them here.

A few highlights about my coin collection:

  • ☝ There are 58 regions, including no longer exsited regions (Yugoslavia, Czechslovakia, etc.).
  • ✌ The oldest coin I own is French 50-centime coin (1931).

I also extend my collection by collecting banknotes. The total number of unique regions for accounting banknotes and coins is 89.
