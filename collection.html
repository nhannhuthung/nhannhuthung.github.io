Remark

I started my collection with only coins and I continue collecting them until now. However, since there are too many - more than 650 coins, I couldn't put all of them here.

A few highlights about my coin collection:

  • ☝ There are around 60 regions, including no longer exsited regions (Yugoslavia, Czechslovakia, French Indochina etc.).
  • ✌ The oldest coins I own are American 1-dollar (1881) and French Indochinese 1-piastre (1902).

My collection is extending with banknotes.
